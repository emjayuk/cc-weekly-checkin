Welcome.

Most couples go days — sometimes weeks — without ever really asking each other: "How are you doing? Truly?"

Not the surface answer. The honest one.

The Weekly Emotional Check-In gives both of you a quiet, private space to answer four short prompts each week. When both partners submit, a personalised Conversation Guide is generated — built entirely from your responses — to help you start a real conversation.

No accounts. No subscriptions. No data ever leaving your device. Just the two of you, showing up for each other, one week at a time.

Everything you need is in your download. This guide walks you through getting started in three minutes.

How To Start

The app has three tabs: This Week, Our Guide, and History. Here's the flow:

  • 1
    Open the app and go to "This Week"
    The current week's date is already shown at the top. You'll see two cards — one for each partner.
  • 2
    Partner 1 fills in their check-in
    Answer the four prompts honestly. The energy slider (1–10) captures how you're feeling this week overall. Tap "Submit My Check-In" when done.
  • 3
    Pass the screen to Partner 2
    Partner 1's answers are now locked. Partner 2 fills in their own responses privately, then submits.
  • 4
    Open "Our Guide" and start your conversation
    Once both partners have submitted, the Conversation Guide unlocks. It shows your shared appreciations, gentle prompts for anything that hurt, what each of you needs right now, your energy comparison, and a suggested conversation starter.
When to do your check-in

Any time works — but Sunday evening or Monday morning tends to feel natural. Pick a time that becomes your weekly ritual and protect it.

Privacy

What you share in this app is deeply personal. It was built with one principle: your data belongs to you alone.

  • Nothing leaves your device. All responses are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. No servers. No cloud. No syncing.
  • No account required. There is no login, no email address, no profile. The app opens and works immediately.
  • Works fully offline. Once the page has loaded once, the app works with no internet connection. Your responses never travel over a network.
  • Data is stored in your browser. If you clear your browser storage or use a different browser, previous check-ins will not appear. Use the same browser on the same device to keep your history.
  • No analytics or tracking. There is no tracking code, no advertising pixels, and no third-party scripts collecting data about how you use the app.

Common questions

The Conversation Guide isn't showing.
Both partners need to tap "Submit My Check-In" before the guide unlocks. Check that both cards show the green "Done ✓" badge.

My history disappeared.
History is stored in your browser. Use the same browser and device each week to keep your history intact.

The app isn't opening.
Make sure you're opening index.html directly in a web browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox or Edge). Do not try to open it with a text editor.
